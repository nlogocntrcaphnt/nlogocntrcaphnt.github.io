What to say..

>savants propelling progress in a field exponentially by laying down the fundamentals out of an aristocratic sense of curiosity and exploration
>lesser men motivated by mostly material aspirations proceed to expand on the foundation but perverting it (lesser because it's easier to build upon, in the same way that it's easier to get richer if already rich)
>material wealth generated breeds convenience and complacency, further suppressing and stifling those capable
>there comes a point, probably black swan type event or maxxed out potential of foundation, when the basis must necessarily be revisited, except there's no one capable of even re-creating it left, let alone improving upon it
